HERE ARE THE TEAM HIGHLIGHTS!

For the Performance Prize, we will take the five fastest individual times from each team from each of the 5 events (only one time will be counted per person for this competition, so if a racer rides twice, only their fastest time will be used for team competition). After the last stage of the series with the John Fraser Memorial Time Trial, the collected times will be added together, the team with the fastest combined time will be awarded $500!

Fine Print: You cannot switch teams for this competition. For instance, ABD could not ask Christian Vande Velde to be on our team for this series. If you are an unattached rider, we are encouraging you to bring your friends and give your group a name.

INDIVIDUAL COMPETITOR DETAILS!

For the individual competition, there will be an overall first place winner in each category based on fastest combined times of all 5 events. To qualify, a rider will need to participate in all stages of the series and there must be a minimum of 3 competitors in the overall for the age group category. There is over $600 in prizes for this, which will be divided up by the number of qualifying categories.


PRE-REGISTRATION

Registration is on a first-come-first-serve basis, so register ASAP to secure the best possible time for you. Registration closes the week prior to each event online. If you send in the registration form via US Mail, the postage date on the envelope will be used to determine when you registered. If it is postmarked after the closing date for registration, please add an additional $3 for the late fee. If the $3 isn't added and it is postmarked after the closing date, your registration will not be processed.
