PRE-REGISTRATION



Online pre-registration closes at midnight (central time) the Thursday evening before the race. No exceptions. No refunds. No online service charges for any CCC race. $5 late fee for day-of registration. Save time and money by registering online. Day-of registration opens at 7:00am by cash or check only. Registration for each category closes 30 minutes before the start of each race, No exceptions. Show up on time or you will not be allowed to race and you will forfeit your entry to those on the waiting list.
