Points Rules

For the criterium races, the maximum number of points per race will 10. The points are awarded as follows:

  1. 10 points
  2. 9 points
  3. 8 points
  4. 7 points
  5. 6 points
  6. 5 points
  7. 4 points
  8. 3 points
  9. 2 points
  10. 1 points

If there are less than 10 riders in a category, the maximum number of points will be the numbers of riders in the category. For example, if the race had 4 riders in a category, the points would be awarded as follows:

  1. 4 points
  2. 3 points
  3. 2 points
  4. 1 points

  8. 3 points
  9. 2 points

For Team Points only: at the time trial, the fastest man and woman of the day will earn an extra 5 points for their team.

For all events, riders can earn points in one category only. They have to declare, during registration and check in, the category in which they are attempting to earn points for their team. That category will remain the same for the entire Fall Fling and cannot be changed at any point after their first race.


PRE-REGISTRATION

If you do not want to pre-register online, you can download the registration and send it via US Mail. On the entry sheet please list the dates you plan on racing if not doing all 4, as well as the category or categories that you plan on racing.
