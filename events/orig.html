Indoor Time Trial Series

Jan. 12 | Feb. 02 | Feb. 23 | Mar. 15 | Apr. 06
The Mid America Time Trial (MATTS) season-opening events where riders race against the clock & each other on CompuTrainers using their own bicycles. The final event of this series is the outdoor John Fraser Memorial Time Trial

Wednesday Night Training Criterium Races

Every Wednesday eventing from May to October
Training races on an eight-tenth of a mile closed-circuit course for all ages and ability levels

Master's Memorial Day Weekend

May 24 | May 25 | May 26
Master-level races for all riders, aged 30 years and older, at all ability levels

Winfield Criterium Weekend

Jul. 12
One of the more popular criteriums in the Midwest that draws riders of all ages and abilities from other states and countries all cheered on by one of the more cycling-friendly towns in Illinois

Fall Fling

TBD
The final road-racing series of the Midwest for the year where riders of all ages and abilities race for overall prizes including the coveted Team Competition

Sunrise Park Cyclocross

Oct. 26
Stop number 6 in the 13 race Chicago Cyclocross Cup which started in 2004 from local teams that had a strong desire to race in their neighborhood
