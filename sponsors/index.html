Prairie Path Cycles has been the major supporter of Athletes By Design since day 1, providing products and services to each and every member. Prairie Path Cycles wants to make sure that you have a bike you will enjoy riding for years to come. Their experienced staff will help you find the bike that is perfect for your style of riding and your budget. They will sell you the bike you need and the bike you'll love!


www.prairiepathcycles.com

Trek, a Midwest-based company, is another supporter of Athletes By Design since the beginning. Trek is one of the biggest bicycle manufacturers in the world, and has been since 1979. Their mission back then was simple: Build the best bikes in the world. Today, they've added to that mission: Help the world use the bicycle as a simple solution to complex problems. They believe in bikes! Check out their entire line of bicycles at Prairie Path Cycles.


www.trekbikes.com

Bontrager has been supporting Athletes By Design for many years. Bontrager’s existence is ultimately linked to the exploration and enjoyment of playground earth while riding on two wheels. Bontrager makes bicycle components, assessories and apparel, all firmly grounded in no-nonsense design philosophy, valuing performance and usability, not fads and gimmicks. Check out Bontrager’s entire line of components, accessories, and apparel at Prairie Path Cycles.


www.bontrager.com

Pactimo has been supporting Athletes By Design since 2011. Pactimo has found continuous inspiration in their home’s breath-taking beauty and extreme conditions to develop cycling apparel for every climate, elevation, and adventure since 2003. Pactimo’s product development is driven by continuous direct feedback from thousands of customers, as well as by personal testing in the rugged and diverse conditions of their backyard.


www.pactimo.com

Athletes By Design’s members believe in the club so much they even help support the team. Orange Home Services prefers to offer their clients the experience of having a more personable and customizable experience.


www.orangehomeservices.com
