Joe Berenyi wins GOLD in the Kilo TT at the UCI Para-Cycling Track World Championship!

Today in the C3 Kilo TT at the UCI Para-Cycling Worlds in Apeldoorn Netherlands, Joe Berenyi outclassed the field by 1.3 seconds to win the rainbow jersey! Joe wasn't the fastest over the first 250 meters but began to wind it up with the fastest 250 meter splits for the remainder of the kilo!
