--- layout: default title: "2015 Race Season is Almost in the Books" slug: news date: 2015-10-07 10:37:00 -0500 ---

First off, a huge thank you to everyone that came out to our Fall Fling, racers and volunteers!

Congratulations to all of the winners, including the 2015 Fall Fling Team Winner, Tower Racing, claiming the $1,000 prize. WDT and ABD tried to put up a fight, but Tower was just to much to handle this fall. Imagine if teams like EGO, PLUS, Bicycle Heaven, and more were able to field 5 riders every event, it would have been a much closer fight to the finish for sure.

ABD would also like to thank Lisa Pena, Scott Witt, Bob Lundberg, and Rich Johnson for hosting the summer's Practice Crits. If you have not joined us, plan on attending next years races, every Wednesday night!

Thank you once again to all of the racers and the volunteers!
