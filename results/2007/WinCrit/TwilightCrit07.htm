2007 Winfield Twilight Criterium Results

Over 200 riders braved the hills and house parties of Winfield's North Side neighborhood at this year's Twilight Criterium. A special thanks to Winfield resident Tim Allen for organizing the biggest party and special "King of the Hill" prizes including fistfulls of cash and iPod's!

Most importantly, thanks to all of the ABD Club Members & Sponsors who made the event possible!
