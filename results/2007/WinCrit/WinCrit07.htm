2007 ABR National Criterium Championship Results

400 riders competed in the 2007 ABR National Criterium Championship held under mostly sunny skies (with one brief downpour) in Winfield, Illinois.

Special Recognition goes to Gary Gall & Main Street Winfield, the Village of Winfield, and WEMA for all their work and support to make this event happen.

Most importantly, the ABR National Criterium Championship wouldn't be possible without the tireless dedication of the ABD Club Members, who donate hundreds of volunteer hours to this event every year.
